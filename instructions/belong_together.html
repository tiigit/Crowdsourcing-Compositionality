How to perform the task:

  1. Read the task instructions below and make sure you understand them before starting the task.
  2. Perform the task according to the instructions.
  3. Click "Submit" to submit the completed tasks.

Payment information: You will be paid $0.2 per task suite. You will be paid immediately after the task suite is completed and submitted.

Purpose statement: These tasks are part of a research project conducted at the University of Helsinki.

Instructions:

You will see a science-themed diagram (see the example pictures below).

The diagram contains different parts, such as text, arrows and illustrations.

Some parts are highlighted in red. Other parts are highlighted in green.

Your task is to decide if the red part forms a group with some green part.
  1. Use the zoom tool to make sure you can see the whole diagram.
  2. Find the part highlighted in red.
  3. Answer the following question: does the red part form a group with some part highlighted in green?
  4. If the red part forms a group with some part highlighted in green, answer Yes.
    If the red part does not form a group with some part highlighted in green, answer No.
